Indie Games To Check Out

  • Deep Rock Galatic is a cooperative first-person shooter developed by Ghost Ship Games. In this game, players embark on missions in fully generated cave systems. Each mission varies in terrain, objectives, hazards, and enemies depending on the selected biome and mission type.

    Sydney enjoys Deep Rock Galactic for its gem mining, bug shooting, and easy-to-join gameplay. The community is friendly and welcoming, making the experience even more enjoyable.

  • Valheim is a third-person, open-world survival game developed by Iron Gate Studio and inspired by Norse mythology, where players take on the role of a fallen Viking. They must fight enemies, craft tools, and build shelters to survive.

    Sydney loved the game's unique crafting system and its distinct art style, which employs low-polygon models and vibrant lighting to create an immersive environment. The ambient music that plays as players traverse the plains is peaceful, and Sydney hopes to see more additions to the soundtrack. She finds that playing with others and progressing together makes the game even more enjoyable.

  • Hades a rogue-like dungeon crawler developed by the creators of Bastion and Transistor, where players challenge the god of death while hacking and slashing their way through the Underworld of Greek mythology.

    Sydney appreciates the addictive gameplay and the rewarding experience of fighting through the stages. She also enjoys the narrative based on how you play, as well as the art style and voice acting.

  • Spiritfarer is a cozy management game developed by Thunder Lotus Games, centered around the theme of dying. Players take on the role of the ferrymaster for the deceased, building a boat to explore the world, care for spirit friends, and ultimately guide them into the afterlife.

    Sydney loves how the game beautifully presented mental health, grief, and slice-of-life themes. She thinks it's a unique experience filled with emotions, one that players can take their time rather rush through. For players that enjoy a slow-paced with easy gameplay, this is for you.

  • Another Crab's Treasure is a Soulslike action-adventure video game developed by Aggro Crab and played from a third-person perspective. In this game, players control Kril, a hermit crab stripped of his protective shell.

    Sydney has been meaning to play this cute and surprising game and has heard that, unlike most Soulslike titles, its aesthetic is bright, cartoony, and playful.

TV Show Recommendations

Scavengers Reign

An intense and original animated sci-fi series streaming on Netflix, follows a group of interstellar scavengers as they navigate a mysterious planet filled with bizarre creatures and landscapers, uncovering secrets from their pasts.

Vinland Saga

A gripping historical anime that follows the journey of Thorfinn, a young Viking warrior seeking revenge against his father's killer while grappling with themes of honor, revenge, and the harsh realities of war during the age of the Vikings.

Mr and Mrs Smith

A thrilling action-comedy on Amazon Prime that shows the lives of John and Jane Smith, a seemingly ordinary married couple who are secretly skilled assassins working for competing agencies.

Andor

A gritty Disney Star Wars series that follows Cassian Andor's journey from disillusioned thief to a key Rebel Alliance figure, exploring the darker side of rebellion, sacrifice, and espionage in a richly detailed galaxy.

The Bear

One of Hulu's intense drama set in a fast-paced kitchen that showcases a young chef returning home to run his family's sandwich shop,
navigating a high-pressure kitchen life while dealing with personal and professional struggles. Highly recommend.
